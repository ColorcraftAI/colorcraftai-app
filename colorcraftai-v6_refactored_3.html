Everything you want to know
Does the AI reply automatically without my approval? ▾

You choose. Enable full auto-reply mode (AI posts immediately) or approval mode (AI drafts, you approve with one tap on WhatsApp). Change anytime from the dashboard.

What languages does the AI reply in? ▾

The AI detects whether the reviewer wrote in Hindi, English, or Hinglish and replies in the same language automatically. Every reply is personalised to the specific review — never a copy-paste template.

Is the ₹999 Scan a one-time payment or monthly? ▾

One-time only. You pay ₹999 once and receive your full Reputation Scan report — no subscription, no recurring charges. If you want ongoing monitoring and AI replies, upgrade to Full AI Management (₹2,999/month).

Do I get a GST invoice? ▾

Yes. Every payment — one-time scan or monthly subscription — automatically generates a proper GST invoice sent to your email. Ready for your business accounting.

How does the WhatsApp alert work? ▾

We use the official WhatsApp Business API via an authorised Meta Business Partner. The moment a new review is detected, a formatted WhatsApp arrives on your number with the full review content and a one-tap AI reply button. No app to install.
