Our mission

Every Indian business deserves a fair chance to be discovered online. We use AI to level the playing field — giving local clinics, restaurants, hotels, and coaching centres the same reputation management tools that large brands have.

Our story

How ColorcraftAI was born

We noticed that thousands of excellent Indian businesses were losing customers to competitors with worse products — simply because their Google ratings didn't reflect their quality. A 3.8★ dental clinic with brilliant doctors was being passed over for a 4.3★ competitor. A family restaurant with 20 years of history was invisible in local search.

The problem wasn't the business — it was the reviews going unanswered, the bad reviews sitting there for months without a response, and the happy customers who never thought to leave a review.

We built ColorcraftAI to fix that. Our AI monitors every review, drafts professional responses in Hindi, English and Hinglish, and helps businesses build the kind of consistent reputation that earns them more customers every month.

We're based in Faridabad, Haryana, and we serve businesses across India.
