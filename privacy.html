Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025 · Effective immediately

ColorcraftAI ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user or client of our Google reputation management service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Business information — name, city, business type, contact email and phone number provided when you sign up
  • Google Business Profile data — your public Google reviews, ratings, and listing information, accessed via Google's API with your authorisation
  • Usage data — how you interact with your client dashboard, which features you use, and chatbot conversations
  • Payment information — billing details processed securely by our payment provider. We do not store card numbers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To monitor and respond to your Google reviews on your behalf
  • To generate AI-powered review responses using Claude (Anthropic)
  • To provide your reputation dashboard and weekly reports
  • To improve our AI chatbot responses over time (anonymised)
  • To send you billing invoices and service updates

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate ColorcraftAI:

  • Google APIs — to access and respond to your Google reviews
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — to generate review responses and power our chatbot
  • Supabase — to store your review and client data securely in India-region servers
  • Vercel — to host and serve our platform

Each provider has their own privacy policy. We share only the minimum data necessary to operate the service.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure, encrypted servers. We use industry-standard practices including HTTPS encryption and access controls. Your Google reviews data is never sold or shared with third parties outside of service delivery.

5. Your Rights

  • Access — Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — Ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your data when you cancel
  • Portability — Export your review history at any time from your dashboard

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you cancel, we delete your personal data within 30 days. Anonymised, aggregated usage data may be retained longer to improve our service.

7. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at [email protected]. We respond within 2 business days.
